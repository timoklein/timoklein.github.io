Research / Publications

My research interest lies in the development of robust and sample efficient reinforcement learning algorithms as well as their application to high leverage societal issues.
In the past, I have been working on planning algorithms for autonomous vehicles in multi-agent settings. Before that I have also done work in information retrieval from heterogenous documents.
